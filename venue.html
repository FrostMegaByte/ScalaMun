Venue

Opening Ceremony

The fifth edition of ScalaMUN will start off in the political heart of the city of Alphen aan den Rijn! The opening ceremony will be hosted in the Town Hall. Right at the place where important decisions are made, our debate will start.


Conference

The venue of the ScalaMUN 2017 conference will be one of Scala College`s locations. This will be the building called `Sacharov`, located at the Dr. A.D. Sacharovlaan 1 in Alphen aan den Rijn. It is a very convenient building that is close to Alphen aan den Rijn station. It is therefore easy to reach. To get to Sacharov, take the train from anywhere to Alphen aan den Rijn. From there on all that remains to get to the building is a five-minute-walk.

Sacharov is also close to the centre of Alphen aan den Rijn, where a shopping mall can be found. Are all your tights worn-out and do you want to buy new ones? Do you still need to buy some cookies to bribe your chairs or reward your admins? No worries! You can easily walk from Sacharov to the centre or take the bus if you find that more convenient. You will be there in a heartbeat!

Our team is willing to pick you up from your bus stop or the train station, if you let us know your arrival times in advance!
